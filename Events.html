Featured Event

23 OCT 2021 Dallas Alumni Reunion

CVR College of Engineeting organizes Dallas Alumni Reunion to celebrate and bring together the Dallas Alumni, Community, and Professional Students involved in the advancement of engineering engineering in Dallas and Dallas communities. All of our classes consist of an eclectic mixture of student and teacher presentations, lectures, exercises, and workshops. As a result of this, many of the students at Alumni Reunion are invited to join Alumni for their classes. The Dallas Alumni, with its welcoming atmosphere and diverse faculty, will share their experiences of being members of Dallas Alumni Reunion and the Dallas alumnus movement.
